About ROS DJ

Rostyslav Dabizha — Ukrainian DJ and music producer. ROS DJ crafts deep, melodic and atmospheric Organic House sets that guide listeners through immersive soundscapes. Since the beginning of the war in Ukraine, he relocated to Spain and continues to captivate audiences with a unique track selection and mixing style.

Genres: Organic House · Deep House · Melodic
